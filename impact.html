Success Stories

Real-world examples of heritage-tech making a difference

Adinkra Symbols in Digital Design

Traditional Adinkra symbols from Ghana have been digitized and incorporated into a design platform used by over 10,000 creatives worldwide.

Impact: Generated $2.3M in licensing fees for Ghanaian communities in 2022 alone.

Maasai IoT Livestock Management

Combining traditional Maasai livestock knowledge with IoT sensors and satellite data to create a smart herding system.

Impact: Reduced livestock losses by 40% and increased herder incomes by 60%.

West African Textile Platform

A blockchain-based platform that authenticates and licenses traditional textile patterns for global fashion brands.

Impact: Created 500+ jobs and increased artisan incomes by 300% in participating communities.
