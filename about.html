About Heritage-Tech

Bridging Africa's rich cultural heritage with the opportunities of the digital age to create unique intellectual property.

Our Story

Heritage-Tech was founded in 2018 with a vision to preserve and enhance Africa's rich cultural heritage through technology. We recognized that traditional knowledge systems were at risk of being lost, while simultaneously holding immense potential for innovation and economic development.

Our journey began with a simple question: How can we use modern technology to not just preserve, but also enhance and monetize Africa's traditional knowledge for the benefit of current and future generations?

Today, we work across 15 African countries, partnering with communities, innovators, and investors to create sustainable heritage-tech solutions that generate economic value while preserving cultural identity.

Our Mission & Vision

Guiding principles that drive our work

Our Mission

To empower African communities by applying technology to traditional knowledge, creating unique intellectual property that drives innovation, preserves culture, and generates sustainable economic value.

Our Vision

A future where Africa's cultural heritage is not only preserved but actively contributes to global innovation, with African intellectual property at the forefront of the digital economy.

Our Values

Respect for traditional knowledge, innovation through collaboration, ethical technology application, and sustainable community development.
