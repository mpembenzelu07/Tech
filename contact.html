Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about heritage-tech and how to get involved

What is heritage-tech?
Heritage-tech is the application of modern technology to traditional knowledge and cultural heritage to create unique intellectual property, preserve endangered practices, and generate sustainable economic value for communities.
How can I submit a heritage-tech project idea?
You can submit project ideas through our contact form above, email us directly at innovations@heritage-tech.africa, or attend one of our monthly innovation workshops held across our regional offices.
Do you provide funding for heritage-tech projects?
Yes, we offer grants, seed funding, and investment opportunities for promising heritage-tech projects. Funding ranges from $5,000 for early-stage ideas to $500,000 for scalable projects with proven impact.
How do you protect traditional knowledge holders' rights?
We use blockchain for provenance tracking, create legally binding benefit-sharing agreements, and ensure traditional knowledge holders retain ownership while licensing usage rights. Our IP framework prioritizes community ownership and fair compensation.
Can I volunteer or intern with Heritage-Tech?
Absolutely! We offer volunteer positions, internships, and fellowship programs for students, researchers, and professionals interested in heritage-tech. Visit our careers page or contact us for current opportunities.
